About

I'm  fortunate to be able to share my passion for golf with players of all skill levels.
‍
Fresno has been my home for over a decade beginning with a high school golf career at Clovis North. I'm lucky that I can teach golf for a living.

As a golf coach I will help players develop a passion for the game. I consider building the character of the person is just as important  as developing him the skills.

I will teach my players through my words and action:  respect, integrity and sportsmanship.
